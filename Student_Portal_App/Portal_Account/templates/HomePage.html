EMSI IS A MEMBER OF THE 1st PAN-AFRICAN PRIVATE HIGHER EDUCATION NETWORK HONORIS UNITED UNIVERSITIES OFFICIALLY SINCE JANUARY 2018.

Since its creation in 1986, the Moroccan School of Engineering Sciences has focused its educational project on the quality of its training and on the high employability of its graduates. The academic excellence of its faculty, its observatory of professions and the integration of technological developments have contributed to establishing its notoriety. The school takes a strong interest in research, development and innovation. It makes every effort to carry loud and clear the ambitions and ideals of our country in terms of invention. In this sense, it aspires to continue to represent it honorably in prestigious international events. For 34 years at the service of the Moroccan economy, EMSI aims to contribute to the development of the continent and the training of future Pan-African leaders.
